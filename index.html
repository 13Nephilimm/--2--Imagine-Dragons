Imagine Dragons is a Grammy award-winning alternative rock band which formed in 2008 in Las Vegas, Nevada, United States. The band consists of Dan Reynolds, Wayne Sermon, Ben McKee and Daniel Platzman. Today's statistics shows that Imagine Dragons have sold 35 million RIAA certified singles in the US and 20 million albums worldwide.
